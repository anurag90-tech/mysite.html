What is HACKING in brief ?
The New Age : - HACKING


In today's world ,everyone's work is done digitally.Which has many advantages, but also has many disadvantages .So,one of them is Hacking.






The term "Hacking"means to exploit a computer system or a private network by identifying its weaknesses to gain unauthorized access. Hacking is also a cybercrime ,when it is done without permission .The person who finds weaknesses on the computer system/or a network to gain access is known as a Hacker. There are mainly three types of hacker's:-






1) White Hat : White Hat hacker's are also known as Ethical Hacker. They're good hacker's. White Hat hacker's are authorised hacker's .They use their hacking skills to improve and protect the digital world. They work for many companies by finding bugs ( viruses ) on their software's . Their work is to protect and improve the computer security before malicious hackers detect and exploit them .They work for many companies by finding bugs ( viruses ) on their software's. Hacker's like. Steve Woznaik ( USA ), Ankit Fadia (INDIA) are two famous Ethical hacker's.








2) Grey Hat : Grey Hat hackers are neither so good nor too bad hacker. They are Neutral .Hacker's like : Adrian Lamo (USA),Albert Gonzalez ( USA ) are known as Grey Hat hacker's. Adrian LamoHacked several high-profile computer networks, including those of The New York Times, Yahoo!, and Microsoft. His nickname is Homeless hacker.




3) Black Hat : Black Hat hacker's are the bad hacker's, they violates computer security for personal gain or maliciousness .They use their skills illegally in bad ways like they break into secure network's to destroy,modify,or steal data, or to make the network unusable for authorised network users. Anonymous is Largest group of Black Hat hacker's. No-one knows who is the leader of that group. Kevin Mitnick is the world's most famous hacker. Once he was FBI's most wanted because he hacked into 40 major Corporation's.
